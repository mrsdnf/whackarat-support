# WhackARat Support

Thanks for playing WhackARat!

## About the Game

WhackARat is a fast-paced arcade game with three hilarious themes:

- **Christmas Rats** - Whack rats in Christmas hats, avoid Santa and the elves!
- **Family Chaos** - Smash alarm clocks, dodge dirty diapers!
- **Developer Life** - Squash bugs, dismiss Windows updates, don't whack the UX designer!

## FAQ

**Q: How do I reset my high scores?**
A: Delete and reinstall the app.

**Q: The game is running slow**
A: Try closing other apps and restarting your device.

**Q: How do I change the difficulty?**
A: Select Easy, Normal, or Extreme when choosing a theme.

## Privacy Policy

WhackARat does not collect any personal data. All high scores are stored locally on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
